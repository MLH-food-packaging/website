MHL

We are a business dedicated to removing single use packaging from the food market

With our help even lower-end food businesses can use more sustainable packaging.

Currently sustainable packaging exists, but its only available on some more expensive products. Our aim is to make sustainable or recycled packaging affordable for even the lower-end food products, and to make the sustainable option the better option.

Our products

For UK based companies

In the United Kingdom the government recently announced a new law proposal that would introduce tax penalties to companies using single-use plastics. As a UK based company it is our duty to give aid to businesses affected by this law and help them in their transition to recycled and sustainable products.

If you or your company is affected, be sure to reach out to us!
